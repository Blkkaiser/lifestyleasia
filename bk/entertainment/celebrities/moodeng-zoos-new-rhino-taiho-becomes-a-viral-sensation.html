Moodeng Zoo’s New Rhino Taiho Becomes a Viral Sensation.

Updated: Nov 28, 2024 6:20 PM EST  | 
3 min read
By Eric E. Surbano
Share this article
FacebookLineTwitterEmail

Moodeng Zoo has a new star in the spotlight, and it’s none other than Taiho, a young rhino who has captured the hearts of visitors and social media users alike.

Since his arrival last month, Taiho has quickly risen to fame, sparking conversations about whether he might dethrone the zoo’s longtime mascot, the beloved giant tortoise Moodeng. Taiho, a three-year-old white rhino, was brought to Moodeng Zoo as part of a global conservation initiative aimed at raising awareness for endangered species. His playful demeanor, combined with his habit of charging toward visitors playfully before stopping short to nibble on treats, has turned him into an instant fan favorite. “He has so much personality,” said zoo director Sarah Lin. “Every day, Taiho finds a way to charm visitors with his antics.”

[Hero image: Moodeng Zoo’s New Rhino Taiho]

Social media videos of Taiho splashing in his mud pool and playfully interacting with keepers have gone viral

Attracting millions of views and comments praising his energetic and curious nature. The hashtag #TaihoTakeover has gained traction, with fans joking that the rhino is the new king of Moodeng Zoo. While Taiho’s rise has been meteoric, fans of Moodeng, the iconic giant tortoise, are rallying to remind everyone of the zoo’s long-time viral sensation. Moodeng’s slow and steady charm has been the face of the zoo for years, drawing in visitors with his calming presence and a 24/7 livestream of his enclosure. “Moodeng is a legend, but Taiho is bringing a fresh, dynamic energy,” said one visitor.

Zoo officials are thrilled with the newfound attention Taiho has brought. “At the end of the day, it’s about conservation and education,” Lin said.

“Whether it’s Taiho or Moodeng, our goal is to connect people with these incredible animals and inspire action to protect them.”

As Taiho’s popularity continues to grow, the Moodeng Zoo is poised to remain a hub of excitement for animal lovers everywhere.

Note:
The information in this article is accurate as of the date of publication.
